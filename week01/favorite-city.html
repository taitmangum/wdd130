My Favorite City: St. George, Utah

St. George has always been one of my favorite places because it feels completely different from northern Utah. I like the warm weather, red rock scenery, and being close to places like Sand Hollow and Zion National Park.

I also like that there are a lot of things to do outside. Whether it is riding dirt bikes, camping, hiking, or just driving around and exploring, there is always something fun nearby. It feels like a place where you can slow down and enjoy being outside.
